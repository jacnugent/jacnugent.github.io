General tools

  • American Meteorological Society (AMS) glossary (short definitions for all kinds of atmospheric science terms)
  • University of Washington Atmospheric Sciences links:
    • Virtual map room (surface maps, upper air maps, satellite images, radar, and more!)
    • Virtual map room archive
    • Loops from David Ovens (LOTS of observations, maps, etc.)
  • Tropical Tidbits (satellite images, forecast models, and other analysis tools; great for weather outside the US)
  • Windy.com (interactive maps of weather variables and air motion)
  • Storm Prediction Center (SPC) forecast tools (some of these are also linked later on)
  • Weather Prediction Center (WPC) daily weather map (current surface analysis, temperature, and 24 hour precipitation)
  • University Corporation for Atmospheric Research (UCAR) image archive (good archive for historical events)
  • Maps from Alicia Bentley (all different kinds of weather variables and model analyses)
